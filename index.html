Who I Am

A curious engineer, obsessed problem-solver, and someone who believes the best way to learn is to build something real.

Hey — I'm Shivam Raval, a 2nd year B.Tech CSE student at IIIT Vadodara, Gujarat. I'm from Rajkot and I grew up with one obsession that's only gotten stronger over time: solving problems.

For me, coding started as a puzzle game and became a lifestyle. I spend a huge chunk of my time on Codeforces (handle: rshvm9) and LeetCode (handle: NoEnemies_1001) — not because I have to, but because there's something deeply satisfying about cracking a hard problem after hours of grinding. At hackathons, I bring the same mindset I bring to contest problems — understand it deeply, break it down, and execute.
